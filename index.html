About us

We are the African students at Illinois Tech. We are committed to enlightening the Illinois Tech Campus, and chicago at large, about the rich cultures of the different nations that make up our continent. It is also our goal to provide a community where the African students at Illinois Tech can feel at home. We are family. ASO is an inclusive organization - every and any one are welcome to join. We welcome the world.

Mission

  • To share the rich African heritage through community service, cultural events, and collaborations with other organizations.

Vision

  • To promote Africa by empowering ethical and global leadership.
